About me

  • Hi, that's me!

    I'm a {{age}} year old French backend engineer. I have fierce self-learning skills and am a heavy user and promoter of both free (as in freedom) software, and community. I enjoy hacking with distributed systems, Python and bash/zsh, but also languages lower in the stack, like C or Go. I have a strong grasp of what's behind the scene of a web application (especially python): from code to distributed computing, caching, deployment and server administration, I try to be as polyvalent as one can be.

    I currently live in Lyon - France, as a backend engineer for a web agency called Askin.

    I like to talk. A lot. So much, that sometimes, I put it to good use, and give talks (mostly about Python, sometimes Go) in front of people. I focus on introductory talks (about a language, a framework, a library, etc), as I strongly feel that the technical world needs more diversity. I'm happy to try changing that at my humble level by helping people jump the bridge and start coding :)

    I also enjoy tinkering with my Raspberry Pi, cooking, music (playing as much as listening), reading nerdy books and French classics, as well as watching the entire Doctor Who series backwards (all that time travel makes it surprisingly possible).
